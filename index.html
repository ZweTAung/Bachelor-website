Bachelor Thesis 2026

VALOR is a bachelor thesis project given by Kongsberg Defense & Aerospace (KDA) in the spring of 2026.

Short Description

The bachelor thesis will focus on developing a concept for a portable and easy to deploy system capable of detecting and dispatching alerts for First-Person View (FPV) drones. FPV drones are a growing challenge because they are cheap, easy to build, and highly agile, making them hard to detect and stop. Operators control them through live video feeds, allowing precise attacks on vehicles, infrastructure, or people. Their small size and ability to fly low make them difficult for traditional defenses to counter.
